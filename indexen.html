About the Association



Who We Are

The Jordanian Association of Doctors in Germany - (JAD) is a non-profit professional association that aims to gather and represent Jordanian doctors living and working in Germany, and to enhance scientific and professional communication between them. The association seeks to support the academic and professional development of its members, exchange medical expertise, and build bridges of cooperation with medical and scientific institutions in Germany and Jordan. The association is also committed to serving the community and promoting the positive role of the Jordanian doctor on both professional and humanitarian levels.

Our Goals

  • Supporting the professional integration and qualification of Jordanian doctors in Germany.
  • Enhancing scientific and professional exchange between Jordanian and German medical staff.
  • Organizing educational workshops and providing information about language and medical exams.
  • Building a strong professional network for Jordanian doctors in Germany.
